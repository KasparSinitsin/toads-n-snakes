About Us

Toads'n'Snakes is a company specializing in the sale of rare exotic animals such as lizards, snakes, turtles, amphibians, and other fascinating creatures. We proudly offer our customers unique pets that are hard to find in regular pet stores. In our collection, you’ll find not only exotic reptiles but also stunning frogs and axolotls that will become the true highlights of your home. Our pets are ideal companions for exotic lovers ready to create a small corner of nature.

How to Choose a Pet?

Every animal requires a unique approach, and we’re happy to help you make the right choice. We recommend:

  • Learning about the temperature and humidity levels needed for your pet.

  • Determining the size and type of terrarium, aquarium, or other habitat.

  • Ensuring you have the time and willingness to care for the pet throughout its life.

If you have any questions, our specialists are always ready to help and share their expertise.

Our Pets

We offer a wide variety of animals, each carefully selected for health and specific care needs.

We have:

  • Reptiles: rare lizards, snakes, chameleons, iguanas, dragons, and many others.
  • Amphibians: axolotls, frogs, newts, and other fascinating creatures.
  • Turtles: various species of exotic land and aquatic turtles.

All our pets come from verified sources and have been thoroughly prepared for sale.

Why Choose Us?

Quality and Care. We place special importance on the health and well-being of our animals. Every pet is in the caring hands of our specialists, ensuring comfortable living conditions.

Education and Support. We not only sell animals but also provide our customers with all the necessary knowledge for proper pet care. We offer consultations on housing, feeding, and training your new friends.

Delivery. We provide safe delivery throughout Russia and Europe. Your pet will arrive in secure conditions and on time.
